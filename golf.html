Game Rules

Pick 7 golfers before the tournament begins. Each day, your golfers earn points based on how many strokes they beat (or lose to) their playing partners by. After the cut on Friday, your team is trimmed to 5 golfers. Saturday scores are worth 1.5x, and Sunday scores are doubled. If any of your golfers are paired together, only the better score counts for that day.
