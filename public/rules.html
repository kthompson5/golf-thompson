Game Rules

  • Pick 7 golfers before the tournament begins. Rank matters for the weekend cut.
  • Each day, your golfers earn points based on how many strokes they beat (or lose to) their playing partners by.
  • If two of your golfers are paired together, only the better score is counted that day.
  • After the cut on Friday, your team is trimmed to your top 5 (you may manually choose or the top 5 remaining scores will be used).
  • Saturday scores are worth 1.5x points. Sunday scores are worth 2x points.
  • Your tiebreaker is the winning final score (highest is best in this game).
  • If there's still a tie, a coin flip will determine the winner.
